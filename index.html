About Us

Rochi Pure is a leading water purification company committed to providing clean, high-quality purified water. We specialize in bulk water supply for vendors, offices, and households, ensuring a reliable source of safe drinking water for all.

With advanced purification technology and strict quality control, we guarantee water that meets the highest safety standards. Whether for home use, businesses, or large-scale distribution, Rochi Pure is your trusted partner for clean and refreshing water.

We are located in Thika, Salama Estate, off Thika - Garissa Highway, just a few kilometers from Makongeni, Thika.

**Our Mission**

Our mission is to provide safe, affordable, and high-quality purified water to communities, businesses, and vendors. We strive to enhance health and well-being by ensuring consistent access to clean water while maintaining environmental sustainability and exceptional customer service.

**Our Vision**

To be the most trusted and preferred provider of purified water in Kenya, setting the standard for quality, reliability, and sustainability. We envision a future where everyone has access to safe drinking water, contributing to healthier communities and a cleaner environment.
